FeelSync collects the following information to provide you with personalized mental wellness support:

  • Mood Entries: Your daily mood ratings, factors, and notes
  • Activity Data: Game scores, breathing exercise duration, and engagement metrics
  • Usage Patterns: How often you use different features (anonymous)
  • Device Information: Basic technical data to ensure app functionality
  • Optional Profile: Name and email if you choose to provide them

üîê How We Protect Your Data

Your mental health information is extremely sensitive. Here's how we safeguard it:

  • Local Storage: All personal data stays on your device using encrypted browser storage
  • No Cloud Sync: Your mood entries and personal notes never leave your device
  • Anonymous Analytics: Only aggregate, non-identifiable usage statistics are collected
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your personal information
  • Secure Communication: All connections use industry-standard encryption
  • Regular Security Updates: We continuously improve our security measures
